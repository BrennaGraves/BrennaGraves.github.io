Candace Owens is someone I look up to because she models confidence, intelligence, and courage. She strives to enhance our awareness of the reality and intentions behind political agendas happening today.

The heroes that I most admire have the following characteristics.

  • Kind - they act with good intent and have upstanding morals and ethics
  • Outrageously courageous - they disturb the status quo and define a new normal
  • Motivate others - people follow a great leader
  • Intelligent - they are knowledgeable about their cause and are constantly trying to learn more
  • Sacrifice & commitment - their impact usually costs them everything
  • Integrity - they live true to their calling

Currently, Candance embodies these traits to me and I look up to her for guidance during these times.
