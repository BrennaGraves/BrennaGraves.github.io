Political Implications

The political implications of Covid have increased the political divide in American. Since two administrations have been involved in the pandemic, there's been many arguments about its handling between the parties. The response has brought wide-spread awareness to the medical industry, the state, local, and federal powers/roles, and global organizations/governance. Many have called into question the intentions behind those influencing and implementing the rules/guidelines. The pandemic has increased people's awareness of the influence big organizations and governements have on the national and global economy.
